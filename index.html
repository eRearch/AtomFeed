为什么选 AtomFeedWhy AtomFeed

信息主动权，才是第一性原理。Information sovereignty — first principles.

你能刷到哪些文献、哪些内容会被做成推送——这两件事你一件都决定不了。微信公众号是被动端上桌的菜，是被 feed 的信息流。你不是主动在找信息，你是在等信息来找你，而且来的不一定是你真正需要的。 Which papers you see, which ones get turned into posts — you control neither. WeChat feeds are algorithmically curated. You aren't finding information; you're waiting for it to find you. And what arrives isn't necessarily what you need.
01 · 第一性原理First principles

直接来源于期刊本身Straight from the journal

AtomFeed 直接来自期刊源头，没有中间商、没有算法过滤、没有内容创作者的主观选择。你看到的就是原始发表的东西。AtomFeed pulls directly from journals. No middleman, no algorithmic filter, no content creator's selection bias. You see what was actually published.

02 · 你决定什么进来You decide what enters

关键词是你的，不是算法的Your keywords, not theirs

你写"anode-free"，我们匹配 AFLMB、anode-less 等所有同义词。精准度是你经验的函数，不是推荐算法的产物。 "anode-free" auto-matches AFLMB, anode-less. Precision is a function of your expertise, not some black-box model.

03 · 相关性问题Relevance problem

99% 的推送跟你没关系99% of posts aren't relevant to you

大部分公众号推送和你自己的研究方向相关性极差。你肯定有经历：真正与你课题相关的文献，还是得自己主动去搜，因为不是所有内容都会被做成推送。Most posts have nothing to do with your specific research. The papers that actually matter to your work still require a manual search — because not everything gets turned into content.

04 · 信息时效Freshness

每天最新，不是别人消化完剩下的Daily new, not pre-digested leftovers

AtomFeed 每天扫描一次，你在文章刚发表的时候就能看到。用了之后你会明显感受到之前信息的滞后性——很多公众号内容是论文发表一两周后才出。AtomFeed scans daily. You see papers the day they drop. After using it, you'll feel how stale your old sources were — most posts appear 1-2 weeks after publication.
